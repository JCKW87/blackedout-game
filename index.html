How scoring works

The game compares your guess to the target in a perceptual color space (CIELAB), then measures the perceptual distance as Delta E (CIE76).

A smooth curve converts that distance into a base score out of 10. Then the scoring system adjusts for hue correctness (warm vs cool blacks) and applies a small penalty when the hue family is off.

The final round score is the base score plus a “recovery” bonus and minus a “hue penalty”, all clamped between 0 and 10.
